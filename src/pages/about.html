About Us
The word "Ludenwright" derives from the latin word Luden, meaning "play", and the Olde English word Wright, meaning "maker" or "builder". Coming together, this word informs our philosophy of "play makers" and creators who build experiences that inspire, challenge, and immerse.

At Ludenwright, we believe in the power of play. As such, we strive to create a community that fosters creativity and empowers indie game developers' commitment to crafting interactive worlds.

Our Mission
Ludenwright’s mission is to empower game developers by providing a place where they can learn, network, and create. We achieve this through education, community collaboration, and guidance.
Building Independence and Fostering Creativity
We provide tools, resources, and a supportive space for you to express your vision and claim your place in the gaming world. Our goal is to help you engage with this thriving industry on your terms.
Championing Cultural Contribution
Your ideas matter. We’re committed to fostering spaces where creative expression knows no limits and innovative concepts take root and flourish.
Providing a Gateway to the Industry
We’re creating pathways for you to confidently establish yourself as a game industry professional. Through active participation and contribution, we’re empowering you to own your role in this dynamic field.
Leading Through Service
Our facilitators and team members prioritize your growth, offering support and mentorship that redefine leadership as collaboration and mutual success.
Cultivating a Healthy Working Environment
We’re actively building a culture that values you for your whole self, not just your work. Our community promotes inclusivity, collaboration, and respect, ensuring that every voice is heard and valued. Being part of the community means being able to practice the values that you want to see in the greater game’s industry as the norm, not the exception.
What Makes Us Unique
We’re building the activities we organize at Ludenwright around Self-Determination Theory (SDT), which highlights the importance of:

Autonomy

Your ability to shape your own path and make meaningful choices.

Competence

Gaining mastery through growth and learning.

Relatedness

Connecting with others and building relationships that matter.

We integrate these principles to cultivate a community where everyone can thrive and create freely. With features like automated guidance systems, mentorship opportunities, and a commitment to openness, Ludenwright stands apart as a hub for game creators to gather, network, and succeed.
